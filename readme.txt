Allow readers to highlight and bookmark POSTs with simply highlight buttons. Easy to add personal notes on POSTs and share.

== Description ==

Highlight Bookmark Manager helps you keep visitors staying longer on your website. By simply adding 3 buttons, your visitors are able to highlight your content & share to social medias. They can also see others public highlights then trigger conversations easily. Building a community has never been easier!

= Increase engagement and traffic =
Want your visitors to stay longer & engage more with the content? Highlight Bookmark Manager grants your wishes with simply 3 buttons:

* Bookmark: bookmark favorite articles and lead visitors come back to your website easily.
* Highlight: highlight and share important parts to social media which increases the traffic at the same time.
* Chatroom: the IM Style Comment System allows visitors to invite friends to join a private or public discussion. Easy to add personal notes on POSTs, too.

= Build a community on your WordPress easily =
Visitors can see the public highlights made by other people and give comments on IM style comment system. Conversations are easily triggered and all happened on your websites! The Highlight Bookmark Manager is free of charge, even the add-ons we sell come with a 14 day money back guarantee!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/highlight_bookmark_manager` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings->Highlight Bookmark Manager screen to configure the plugin
